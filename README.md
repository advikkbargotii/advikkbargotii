### About Me

I'm a Computer Science senior at the **University of Arizona** (graduating May 2026) based in **New York, NY**. I build things at the intersection of AI and software engineering — from LLM-powered automation pipelines to full-stack web applications.

Currently, I'm an **AI Automations Engineer Intern** at American Medical Compliance and a **Co-Founding Engineer** at SAAQ, an AI-driven emotional awareness platform. I also contribute to **surgical training research** at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering.

### What I'm Working On

- **Secura** — AI security agents for automated vulnerability detection & patching using LangChain, FAISS, and custom RAG pipelines
- **SAAQ Platform** — Architecting the frontend and core analysis flows for an AI-based self-awareness platform
- **CAST III** — Enhancing real-time motion tracking and simulation fidelity for laparoscopic surgery training
